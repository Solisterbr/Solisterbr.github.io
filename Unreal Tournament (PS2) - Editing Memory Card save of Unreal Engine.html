Unreal Tournament (PS2) - Editing Memory Card save of Unreal Engine

Post time:

27/09/2016 18:58:46

Hi, you probably knows the Unreal Tournament series, right? Well, but something that few peoples knows, are that exist a PS2 game. The game was released in CD in 2000 (One year after the first game release for PC).

My Unreal Tournament CD (Pirate)

But before start to do what is write in the title, lets do a check-list of what we have and don't have.
  • PlayStation 2
  • Dual Shock 2 (1-player)
  • Unreal Tournament (PS2)
  • Launch Elf
  • TV

First, check if the PlayStation 2 and the TV are turned on and working. After, put the Unreal Tournament CD in PS2. After that and the game started and you're in the Title Screen, create a new save game (If you don't have), and certify that it saved into your Memory Card.


PS2 Memory Card Example (With a bad image quality, Sorry!)

After certified that your game was saved into your Memory Card, Turn Off your PS2 and put the Launch Elf CD. After that, you'll need to access many folders. To do that, I decided to put it in images.

Launch Elf initial page

Press the O (Circle), to access the Launch Elf

Go down to MISC

Press O (Circle) again

Select the TextEditor, and press O (Circle again)

Select Open and again you will go to this screen.

Now, Access your Memory Card (mc0 to Memory Card 1 and mc1 to Memory Card 2).

Now you'll enter into a similar screen like that. Search for BASLUS-20034, this is the fold where the game is saved.

This is the folder (Opened) where the game is saved. Press O (Circle) to finally open the Save Game. The next three screens are related to the Save Game file.

What really interest are at the final of this text. You can use the R2 button to come to the end more fast.

Alright, I came to the final of the page, and now? Easily you understand the file and will can edit it without my help. Starting from the second image (the first are only the text start, doesn't matter to us), The first part are the SavedGames (PS2SavedGames), You can have at least 8 save games, 0 means nothing, and 1 means exist. In the Player Name, you put the Slots names, that is your Nick or your real name. In Player Char, are the players avatars. Unfortunately doesn't have a correct order, you only can put estimating a number. Fortunately, you can use the Practice Session to have a better view of the avatars. The Skill is the difficult between you and the bots. The rest are your position into the Death Match, Capture the Flag, Assault etc.
